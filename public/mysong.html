Writer(s): Alexander Grant, Benjamin Mckee, Daniel Reynolds, Daniel Sermon, Joshua Mosser
"Believer" was released on February 1, 2017 as the lead single from the band's third studio album, "Evolve".
"The meaning of the song is really reflecting on specific things in my life that were painful and just rising above that, finding a place of perspective where I could be appreciative of the pain in my life and make it my greatest strength." - Dan Reynolds.
This song spent more than a year in Billboard Hot 100, peaking at number four and being one of the best-selling song in the USA in 2017.
The trailer of "Murder on the Orient Express" (2017) features this song.
Dolph Lundgren played a role in the music video.
